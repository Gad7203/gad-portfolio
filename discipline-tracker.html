📋 Project Overview

The Discipline Tracker is a personal project designed to help me build and maintain positive habits. It's based on my core belief that Discipline > Luck. This tool helps me stay accountable and track my progress toward becoming the best version of myself.

🎯 Purpose & Motivation

I created this tracker because I believe that success isn't about luck - it's about consistent, disciplined action. Every day presents an opportunity to make choices that align with my goals and values.

📊 Habits I Track

  • Morning Routine: Wake up early and start the day with purpose
  • Study Time: Dedicated hours for learning and skill development
  • Physical Exercise: Daily workout or football practice
  • Reading: Continuous learning through books and articles
  • Coding Practice: Improving web development skills daily
  • Reflection: Evening review of the day's progress and lessons
  • Healthy Eating: Making nutritious food choices
  • Sleep Schedule: Maintaining consistent sleep patterns

💡 Core Principles

🎯 Discipline > Luck

🫡 Respect > Attention
